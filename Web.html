Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

Welcome to Sand Matrix.

We believe privacy is simple. This document explains how we handle your information when you play our game on Facebook Instant Games.

1. No Data Collection

Sand Matrix is a standalone game experience. We respect your privacy by adhering to a strict policy regarding your data:

  • We do not collect any personal information (such as your name, email, or phone number).
  • We do not store any user data on external servers.
  • We do not use any third-party analytics tracking tools.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or third parties.

All game progress is either temporary or handled entirely within the Facebook Instant Games context locally on your device.

2. Facebook Instant Games Platform

This game is hosted on the Facebook Instant Games platform. While we (the developers of Sand Matrix) do not collect data, Facebook (Meta) may collect data as outlined in their own policies.

For more information on how Facebook handles data, please refer to the Meta Data Policy.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We do not use tracking cookies. The game may use your device's local storage solely to save your personal high score or game settings locally. This data stays on your device and is not sent to us.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect email addresses, we suggest checking this page occasionally for any changes. Continued use of the game implies acceptance of the policy.
